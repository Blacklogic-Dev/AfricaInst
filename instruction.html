Study Instructions

Students are paired with an AI Assistant to help them learn faster, understand deeper, and gain the ability to create real-world projects.
“An intelligent heart acquires knowledge, and the ear of the wise seeks knowledge.” – Proverbs 18:15

Learning is project-based and includes structured assessments. You must demonstrate the ability to build, explain, and research.
“Commit your work to the Lord, and your plans will be established.” – Proverbs 16:3

The required pass mark is 50%. Certificates are issued at the end of the course and sent directly via email.

Course assessments include:

  1. 2 Weekly Assignments: Each offered at the end of the week.
  2. 1 Individual Project: Apply your skills independently.
  3. 1 Collaborative Project: Work with peers to solve real-world problems.
  4. Final Exam: Conducted after all assignments and projects are marked.
Certificates are awarded upon completion of all assessments. Proof of skill matters.

Opportunities

Students who achieve a pass mark above 70% are eligible for the BlackLogic Technologist Internship.

The internship involves solving real-world problems that BlackLogic Technologies tackles during actual tender projects. Students undergo 6 months of hands-on training and receive a monthly stipend.

Excellence creates opportunity. Remember, this is survival of the finest.

All candidates completing the course receive a Sector Certification approved by BlackLogic Technologies.
This certification comes with a Reference of Skills letter detailing the skills you have gained, enabling you to apply immediately for jobs or further studies.
